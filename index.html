Our Mission

A Small Change builds community stability in Lake City, South Carolina. We connect neighbors to food, resources, and mutual support. We do this consistently, openly, and without barriers.

Our work is grounded in a simple principle: small, consistent actions build lasting change. We are present every week because our neighbors depend on it.

Free Lunch for Folks

Our flagship program delivers free bagged lunches, pantry items, and hydration every week in Lake City. No forms. No requirements. No judgment.

  • Bagged lunches distributed weekly
  • Pantry items included at every distribution
  • Hydration support provided at each event
  • Fixed site and roving site serving the community
  • Approximately 50 lunches served each week
  • Resource referrals available on site
