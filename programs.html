Programs

Direct service programs in Lake City, SC.

Free Lunch for Folks

Free Lunch for Folks is our flagship program. Every week, we distribute free bagged lunches, pantry items, and hydration support to anyone in need in Lake City and the surrounding community. We ask for nothing in return.

No forms. No ID required. No questions asked. If you are hungry, come. If you know someone who is, bring them.

What We Provide

  • Bagged lunches with a nutritious meal
  • Pantry items at each distribution
  • Hydration support at every event
  • Resource referrals to additional services
  • Consistent community presence

How It Works

  • Distributions happen every week, year-round
  • We operate a fixed site in Lake City
  • We also run a roving site to reach more neighbors
  • Approximately 50 lunches are distributed each week
  • Volunteers staff every event

Who We Serve

Anyone. We do not screen participants or require documentation. We serve individuals, families, seniors, and anyone who arrives. Community presence is part of our work. We are here to be seen and to see you.

Resource Referrals

Volunteers at each distribution can connect participants to additional local resources. We maintain relationships with service organizations in Lake City and Florence County. Ask us. We will help you find what you need.

Future Programs

A Small Change is committed to expanding our support as Lake City's needs grow. We are actively listening to our community and developing new programs grounded in the same values that guide Free Lunch for Folks.

Crisis Assistance

We are developing a crisis assistance program to help neighbors facing urgent needs in utilities, housing, and basic necessities.

Resource Navigation

We are building a structured resource navigation service to connect community members to health, housing, and benefits programs throughout Florence County.

Seasonal Programs

We plan seasonal programs to address specific needs throughout the year, including back-to-school support and winter essentials distribution.
