Get Help

Free food and support are available in Lake City. Come as you are.

You Are Welcome Here

A Small Change provides free lunches, pantry items, and assorted cold drinks to anyone in the Lake City area. We do not ask for paperwork. We do not require proof of need. We do not turn people away.

No forms. No ID. No requirements. If you need food or support, come to a distribution. Bring yourself. Bring a neighbor. You are welcome.

What to Expect

Free Bagged Lunches

Bagged lunches are ready to eat. You do not need to prepare anything. In order to serve as many families as possible, we give out 2 lunches per family member and one drink per lunch.

Pantry Items

Pantry items are available at every distribution. Items vary week to week based on donations and supply.

Assorted Cold Drinks

Water and assorted cold drinks are provided at every event. We prioritize this, especially in warm months.

Resource Referrals

Our team can connect you to additional services including housing, utilities assistance, health resources, and benefits programs in Florence County.

Community Presence

Our volunteers are here to talk, listen, and connect. We are a community organization, not just a food line.

No Judgment

We do not evaluate why you need help. We do not ask. We are here to support you, no questions required.

When and Where

Free Lunch for Folks distributes weekly in Greater Lake City, SC Area.

Fixed Site

Our distribution location is in Lake City at the NAPA Auto Parts Parking Lot, Sundays at 1:00 PM.

Other Resources

We can connect you to additional services in Lake City and Florence County. At any distribution, ask a volunteer for information. We can provide guidance to organizations that provide:

  • Emergency housing assistance
  • Utility assistance programs
  • Health and mental health services
  • SNAP and benefits enrollment support
  • Employment services
  • Childcare and family support programs
